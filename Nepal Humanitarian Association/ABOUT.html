Our
Mission

"

In Nepal, one of the most desperate countries for education and training, our activities have started in 2009 in the capital, Kathmandu. In order to provide high-quality education for the children of the country in the region, the Trust has purchased 1037 square meter of land with the help of Nepali benefactors in the city of Kathmandu. In 2012, a building was constructed which has been serving as a student dormitory.  At present, 83 students from the level of secondary and high school are educated.
